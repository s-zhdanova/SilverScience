SilverLens is a student-driven publication created to promote scientific literacy and encourage a deeper understanding of science within the Blair community. Written by and for Blair students, the magazine will be distributed primarily to all Magnet students, with 200 extra copies allocated to STEM Academy classes and the media center, ensuring a wider audience.

Each issue includes 2 articles written by editors:
- A recent science news overview
- A spotlight at a STEM-related activity or club at Blair, suggested by students
And 8 articles taken from student submissions, one in each of the following topics:
- Biology
- Chemistry
- Physics
- Computer science
- Engineering
- Math

Articles not chosen for printing can be found here on this website under the Articles tab.
Fostering science communication, critical thinking, and student engagement in STEM fields, making science accessible and relevant to all.
